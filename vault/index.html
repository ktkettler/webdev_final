THE VAULT

An old concrete and steel vault tucked away in the basement of 431 N Dearborn St. All those that find the way are welcome. The Vault features a menu of new takes on classic cocktails, as well as original creations. The bar features some of the best bartenders in the city, along with guest bartenders from around the globe once a month. We hope you'll be imbibing with us soon.

Service begins at 8pm daily.
